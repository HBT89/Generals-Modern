Generals Modern

This is a community-led effort to modernize and reimagine Command & Conquer: Generals Zero Hour in two major phases:

Phase 1: Modernization Layer

  • Replace the outdated DirectX 8.1 rendering backend with BGFX
  • Update shaders, lighting, and graphical systems for modern GPUs
  • Support for 1440p / 4K / ultrawide resolutions and proper UI scaling
  • Replace deprecated libraries (e.g., Miles Sound System, SafeDisk, etc.)
  • Maintain full compatibility with vanilla Zero Hour gameplay

Phase 2: Full Overhaul Mod

  • Three new core factions: GDP (Global Defense Pact), ESF (Eurasian Strategic Front), and FNC (Autonomous Resistance Collective)
  • Modern drones, cyberwarfare mechanics, and asymmetric doctrines
  • New battlefield layers: VTOLs, naval combat, amphibious assaults
  • Expanded general powers and building-based upgrades

Learn more on the Future Mod Page.

Goals

  • Deliver a modernized C&C experience without losing its roots
  • Enable full modding with modern tools and readable formats
  • Support cross-platform buildability in the long run

This project is open-source and aims to grow with contributions from developers, artists, designers, and fans of Generals alike.
