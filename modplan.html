Generals Modern - Mod Plan

This mod builds on the modernized Generals ZH engine with a bold reimagining of factions, combat mechanics, and geopolitical flavor. A spiritual successor rooted in real-world inspiration.

Factions

GDP – Global Defense Pact

NATO-style force with superior tech, precision tools, and advanced battlefield control.

  • Golden Dome superdefense (anti-drone & missile)
  • 3-charge Precision Cruise Missile superweapon
  • Laser-based anti-drone systems
  • Directed energy and countermeasure upgrades
  • Naval and VTOL support buildings

ESF – Eurasian Strategic Front

BRICS-style coalition that relies on jamming, disruption, and overwhelming force.

  • GPS Scramble Zone (permanent radar blindness)
  • EMP-based artillery and units
  • Tu-160 inspired strategic bomber
  • GPS jammers, power blackouts
  • Heavy reliance on drone air superiority

ARC – Autonomous Resistance Collective

Tech scavengers and insurgents with flexible, swarm-based and covert strategies.

  • Drone Swarm superweapon
  • Underground Logistics Grid (spawns rebel ambushes)
  • Black-market and crypto-based economy
  • Stealth units with random income theft mechanics
  • Cheap, upgradeable infantry with variant skins

Doctrine Framework

Each faction selects one doctrine per battle. Doctrines grant unique unlocks, upgrades, or limit access to specialize gameplay:

  • Land Warfare – Infantry and armor buffs; base-focused land battles
  • Naval Warfare – Access to offshore buildings and drone-carrier platforms
  • Advanced Technology – Access to high-end units, lasers, stealth, AI
  • Cyber Warfare – EMPs, blackouts, stealth, and GPS jamming

Other Planned Features

  • Dynamic infantry variants (skin tone + voice diversity)
  • Multi-light source shaders for realistic lighting
  • Randomized stealth drones and recon patrols
  • Scripted campaign with modern geopolitical themes
  • Modular upgrades to shift base strategy mid-game
